Who We Are

About JMC Engineering

Founded in 2020, JMC Engineering has rapidly evolved into a trusted name in precision engineering. Our commitment to quality, innovation, and transparency enables us to deliver exceptional solutions to clients across diverse industries.

Our CEO brings over 30 years of experience in the Engineering domain (Tool & SPM), including 12 years of expertise in Singapore and 18 years in Chennai — with a strong background in die and tool design, finance, and R&D.

What We Manufacture

Our Services

Engineered with precision, delivered with accountability — every component, every time.

Jig Fixtures
Custom machining and assembly jigs designed for high-accuracy, repeatable production across automotive and industrial applications.
Press Tools
Blanking, piercing, forming, bending, and progressive dies engineered for precision sheet metal operations at scale.
Plastic Moulds
Injection mould tooling for diverse plastic components, designed for dimensional accuracy and long production life.
Gauges & SPM
Inspection gauges, go/no-go gauges, and special purpose machines tailored to your quality control requirements.
Forming Tools
Deep draw, stretch forming, and compound forming tools for complex geometries requiring tight tolerance control.
Engineering Works
Custom precision machining, component development, and prototype manufacturing for diverse engineering requirements.
